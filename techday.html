WHAT IS TECHDAY?

TechDay is a massive science fair for startups. Startups have the opportunity to show off their products to thousands of consumers, the highest caliber investors, hordes of press, the most sought after talent, and the greatest pool of tech enthusiasts looking to celebrate emerging venture. We see Washington D.C. and it’s surrounding areas as the next great startup hub and we want to help foster the growth. So join us for an action packed day that promises to be the best startup event D.C. has ever seen. It’s free to attend….we hope to see you there!

WHO ATTENDS?

Are you an early adopter of tech products? Looking for a job at a startup? Covering the DC tech scene for a publication? Looking to invest in the next Facebook? Or just a plain old tech enthusiast? Then YOU and thousands like you attend TechDay! The Best Part? It’s FREE! BOOM!
